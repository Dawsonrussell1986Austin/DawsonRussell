Frequently Asked

Common questions

What size companies do you work with?

I work with teams of 5 to 500. The sweet spot is growth-stage startups and mid-market companies that want to move fast but need expert guidance on AI infrastructure.

What's the typical engagement timeline?

Most deployments are fully operational within 2 weeks. Training engagements run 3+ months to ensure adoption sticks. I recommend a minimum 3-month commitment for lasting impact.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?

Not at all. I work with technical and non-technical leadership alike. If you're technical, we'll go deep. If not, I'll translate everything into clear business outcomes.

Can you work with our existing tools?

Absolutely. I specialize in integrating AI workflows into your existing stack — whether that's GitHub, Linear, Slack, Notion, or custom internal tools.

Why hire one person instead of an agency?

Because you're paying for results, not overhead. AI lets me deliver at the output level of a full team. You get a senior expert who does the actual work — not a sales rep who delegates to juniors.

What if we're not sure which path is right?

That's exactly what the discovery call is for. I'll assess your team's needs and recommend the engagement that delivers the highest ROI for your specific situation.
